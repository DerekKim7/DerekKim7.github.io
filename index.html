How to find the right weapon for you

Before using any weapon, you have to know whether it is easy or not to use. If you want to find the right weapon, first go on a private match and start trying out weapons. Personally, the easiest weapon is UMP-45 because there aren't a lot of recoil so the bullet goes very straight and direct.

How to be good at the weapon

There are several ways to improve your skills. First, you can go on a private training map or play deathmatch. Deathmatch is a game mode where you can respawn after you die. It is a very good way to train your fast intuition. You can also ask a friend of yours to play a private match and train each other.

Brief Overview

Some weapons I will be talking about

AK-47

The AK-47 is probably the best (automatic) weapon in CS:GO, since it is able to one-shot-kill even with armor, if you hit the head or neck. If you have not yet decided which weapon you want to master first, this is an excellent choice for the terrorist side. It is very effective on all distances, but especially for medium and long distance.

AWP

The AWP is the strongest weapon in CS:GO, since it is able to one-shot-kill even with armor, if you hit the body and head. AWP can be used for both Counter-Terrorist and Terrorist side. I believe it is the best sniper to use.

M4A1-S

The M4A1-S is a very nice gun to use on Counter-Terrorist side. This is because it is a very accurate gun and it can kill someone with only 3 shots even with armor.
